About Our Farm

Alfred's Poultry Farm-Kiambu is a modern, well-managed farm dedicated to raising healthy poultry and producing quality eggs for the local community. Located in the fertile lands of Kiambu, the farm emphasizes proper poultry housing, balanced feeding, and veterinary care to ensure strong, disease-free birds. With a focus on sustainability, Alfred’s Poultry Farm uses hygienic practices in egg collection, storage, and distribution, supplying fresh eggs daily to markets, households, and institutions. The farm also provides training and guidance to aspiring poultry keepers, promoting agribusiness in the region. Its mission is to deliver safe, nutritious poultry products while supporting food security.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Alfred’s Poultry Farm began with just 50 chickens. Today, we are proud to serve Kiambu and Nairobi with over 5,000 layers and broilers.

Alfred’s Poultry Farm began as a small family venture in Kiambu with just a few chickens. Through dedication, innovation, and hard work, it has grown into a trusted source of quality poultry and eggs. The farm’s story reflects resilience, community support, and a vision to promote food security.

Mission & Values

Our mission is to provide affordable, safe, and nutritious poultry products while maintaining sustainable farming practices.

Community Impact

We support local farmers and provide employment opportunities in Kiambu. By supplying schools and hospitals, we contribute to SDG 2.

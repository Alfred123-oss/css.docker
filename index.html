Welcome to Our Farm

Welcome to Alfred’s Poultry Farm in Kiambu, a trusted home of healthy chickens and fresh eggs. We are delighted to share our passion for poultry farming with you. Here, quality meets care as we nurture birds in a clean, safe environment to provide nutritious food for our community.

Who We Are

Located in Kiambu County, Alfred’s Poultry Farm provides HQP in the form of eggs and broilers to the local community and beyond.

We practice sustainable farming while ensuring the welfare of our chickens and delivering fresh and affordable poultry products daily.

Location

The farm is located in Kiambu County, easily accessible from nearby towns and markets.

Products & Services

  • Fresh table eggs (daily supply)
  • Healthy broiler and layer chickens
  • Organic manure from poultry waste
  • Training sessions for smallholder farmers

Farming practices

We use modern poultry housing, clean water systems, balanced feeding programs, and regular veterinary care to ensure quality production.

Market coverage

Our eggs and poultry products serve households, schools, restaurants, and retail shops within Kiambu and beyond.

Sustainability

The farm prioritizes eco-friendly practices, including waste recycling, use of organic fertilizers, and energy-efficient methods.
